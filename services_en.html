Staff development

Our work mostly concerns developing cooperativiness, group dynamics, clarifying roles, finding better work methods, common base values, or finding the balance between effectiviness and long-lasting well-being.

Project management

A&O consulting trains and coaches project managers on IHM Business School's behalf. Project support and developing project models is also part of our repertoire. We are constantly involved in various development and collaborative projects.

Networking

Initiation and revitalisation of professional networks is part of our work.

Management training

Leadership, change management, performance meetings, situational leadership, organisational psychology, motivational work, decision processes and goal orientation are some the of the areas that are of great use for both experienced managers and ambitious management candidates.


Andragogy (adult learning)

Development of interactive, experience-based methods of learning is one of our key competences. An abundance of academic research and practical experience constitutes the foundation for effective and pleasurable learning. We train professional educators and consultants and we do as we teach.


Teambuilding

The knowledge of the team's composition, work methods, life cycle, dynamics and potential pitfalls is extremely valuable within modern organisations. To provide your latest project with a good kick-off, to help the team in preventing unnecessary conflicts and to identify the team's different roles is just the job for A&O consulting.
